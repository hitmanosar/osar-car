About Us

Welcome to the new, calmer way of buying, selling and servicing your car. Welcome to BMW CarStore.


Our Vision

Our vision is to be the UK's favourite automotive retailer. That doesn't just mean you'll get the best experience when you buy a car from us, but when servicing, maintaining and selling your car, too. We believe every part of your car ownership experience should be as tranquil as possible. We'll empower you to make the right decision for you with helpful advice, total transparency and service that's flexible to your needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to act as personal advisors to empower in your car buying, selling and servicing needs. Ever-evolving digital capabilities and CarStore Centres allow you to find the right solution for you, tailoring the car ownership journey to suit your individual needs.
